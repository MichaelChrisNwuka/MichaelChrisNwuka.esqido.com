Application

How to apply like a pro

For the best application, take a q-tip and makeup remover and run it along your lash line to remove any oils from your eyelids.

Then, take your false lashes of choice, and use the Companion lash glue and apply it along the entire lash band until there's a white coat
covering the entire band. Wait a few seconds for glue to become sticky (for the Winter Formula, you may need to wait up to 30 seconds).

Finally, apply the lashes to the base of your natural lash line, and hold for a few seconds for it to dry. Once dried, the glue will no longer be
white and it'll become clear.
